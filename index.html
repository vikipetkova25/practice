About Me

Hello! I'm Victoria Petkova, a dedicated student with a passion for learning and growth. I believe in the power of education to transform lives and create opportunities. My journey is fueled by curiosity and a desire to make a positive impact in the world.

I currently reside in Varna, Bulgaria, and I'm a 16-year-old student at PGKMKS 'Acad. Blagovest Sendov'. With a strong passion for technology and a knack for innovative problem-solving, I like to devise creative solutions. Outside of academics, I enjoy honing my skills in drawing and find inspiration through music. I'm eager to connect and collaborate with like-minded individuals who share my enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of technology and creativity. Feel free to reach out to me on social media!
